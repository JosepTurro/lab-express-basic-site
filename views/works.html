JUJUTSU KAISEN 0 THE MOVIE

The movie comes from the manga where we first met Yuta there. Also later, an anime was made where a story related to that of our character appears. Yuuta Okkotsu is haunted. Ever since his childhood friend Rika died in a traffic accident, her ghost has stayed with him. But his spirit doesn't appear as the sweet girl Yuuta once knew. Instead, he manifests as a monstrous and powerful entity that fiercely protects him. Unable to control Rika's violent behavior, Yuuta is powerless to stop the bloodshed that follows her brutal revenge.
